As the capital of the United Arab Emirates, Abu Dhabi is a popular tourist destination for UAE locals as well as expatriates. The city itself is an island that is situated along the Abu Dhabi Corniche and then spreads inland along picturesque waterways. ABU DHABI ATTRACTIONS

When in Abu Dhabi a visit to Emirates Palace is a must as is the Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque. The mosque is one of the largest in the world so make sure you have your camera ready. Other attractions in Abu Dhabi are Ferrari World, Yas Waterworld, Souk Qaryat Al Beri and Yas Island.

SHOP ABU DHABI

Abu Dhabi’s thriving shopping scene continues to grow and in 2013 it welcomed The Galleria and Sowwah Square. While there are only 13 malls there’s also the Souk Qaryat Al Beri and the Souk at Central Market.

EATING OUT IN ABU DHABI

Most Abu Dhabi restaurants are located in and around hotels but they serve up a mix of cuisine from American to Arabic and everything in between. Most restaurants also offer Halal food, however, it is better to check with the restaurant before going.

TRANSPORT TO AND FROM ABU DHABI AIRPORT

flynas arrives into Terminal 1 at the Abu Dhabi International Airport, which is located 45 minutes from the city centre. Once you’re on the ground the best modes of transport are taxis, buses and cars for hire. Metred taxis are readily available 24 hours a day and the regular flag-fall of AED3 (80c) is applicable from the airport. Buses are also located outside Terminal 1, and leave every 40 minutes 24 hours a day. Bus tickets cost AED3 (80c).

ABU DHABI HOTELS

In Abu Dhabi you’ll find numerous hotel chains from popular 3-star resorts to luxurious 5-star accommodations.

WEATHER IN ABU DHAI

Abu Dhabi is sunny most of the year around, which is one of its main attractions. The best time of year to visit the capital city of the UAE is over the cooler months, from October through to May, with temperatures rising above 50 degrees in summer.

EVENTS IN ABU DHABI

Abu Dhabi is the home of Formula 1 Etihad Airways Abu Dhabi Grand Prix (November). It also hosts several other events throughout the year including the Abu Dhabi HSBC Golf Championship (January), TriYas (February) which is located on Yas Island, Abu Dhabi International Hunting and Equestrian Exhibition (September), Abu Dhabi Film Festival (October), Abu Dhabi Corporate Games (December) and the Mubadala World Tennis Championship (December).
