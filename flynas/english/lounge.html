EXECUTIVE LOUNGES

Looking for a comfortable place to relax at the airport while you wait for your flight? flynas Customers now have the option to enjoy the Executive Lounges at selected international airports from as little as SAR65 ($18), if you are travelling flynas Business Class entry is included in your fare. To book simply go to manage my booking. Alternatively phone our Contact Centre on +966 9200 01234 (toll free KSA only) or +966 114349000 (International), visit a flynas Sales Office or Travel Agent. Once you have selected and paid to use the Executive Lounge, it will appear in the details of your itinerary. When you check in at the airport, our counter staff with issue you with a lounge voucher which you simply to the lounge staff for entry.
